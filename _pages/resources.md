# <center> Data Sources </center>
- - -

Some selected databases for research in Economics, especially useful for studies in Regional and Urban Economics, Economic Demography, Labor Economics and Migration, and Environmental and Ecological Economics.

<br/>

**▸ General, demographic, and socioeconomic databases** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('BLOCK1')">More</a> ] </small>

<div id="BLOCK1" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>

**PNAD Contínua, IBGE** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('PNADC')">More</a> | [Site][PNADCsite] ] <br/>

<div id="PNADC" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Household sample survey with information on labor, income, education, demographic characteristics, and housing conditions. It is widely used in labor economics, inequality, poverty, and public policy evaluation.

Unit of analysis: individual and household  
Periodicity: continuous, with monthly, quarterly, and annual releases, depending on the indicator  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**Censo Demográfico, IBGE** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('CENSO')">More</a> | [Site][CENSOsite] ] <br/>

<div id="CENSO" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Population and housing census of Brazil. It is a classic database for studies in economic demography, regional economics, urbanization, labor markets, and spatial inequality.

Unit of analysis: individual, household, and territorial units  
Periodicity: decennial  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**POF, Pesquisa de Orçamentos Familiares, IBGE** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('POF')">More</a> | [Site][POFsite] ] <br/>

<div id="POF" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Household survey focused on expenditure structure, income, consumption, nutrition, and living conditions. It is widely used in studies on consumption, welfare, poverty, demand elasticities, and food economics.

Unit of analysis: household, individual, and consumption unit  
Periodicity: non continuous, carried out in specific rounds  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**SIDRA, IBGE** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SIDRA')">More</a> | [Site][SIDRAsite] ] <br/>

<div id="SIDRA" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
IBGE system for retrieving statistical tables. It gathers aggregated results from economic, social, and demographic surveys and is especially useful for historical series, territorial comparisons, and the construction of municipal, state, and national panels.

Unit of analysis: statistical aggregates, depending on the survey consulted  
Periodicity: varies according to the statistical operation  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**IPEAData, Ipea** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('IPEADATA')">More</a> | [Site][IPEADATAsite] ] <br/>

<div id="IPEADATA" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Broad database of economic, social, regional, and financial time series, widely used in applied research in Brazil. It is frequently used for inflation, economic activity, labor market, public accounts, and social indicators.

Unit of analysis: aggregated series, with national, regional, state, and municipal breakdowns, depending on the indicator  
Periodicity: varies according to the series  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**SGS, Sistema Gerenciador de Séries Temporais, Banco Central do Brasil** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SGS')">More</a> | [Site][SGSsite] ] <br/>

<div id="SGS" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Economic and financial time series database maintained by the Central Bank of Brazil. It is widely used in macroeconomics, monetary economics, external sector analysis, credit, interest rates, inflation, and public finance.

Unit of analysis: aggregated series  
Periodicity: daily, monthly, quarterly, or annual, depending on the series  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**IRPF, Imposto de Renda da Pessoa Física, Receita Federal do Brasil** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('IRPF')">More</a> | [Site][IRPFsite] ] <br/>

<div id="IRPF" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Tax database with aggregated information from personal income tax returns. It is widely used in studies of inequality, income concentration, top incomes, and tax analysis, especially as a complement to household surveys, which tend to understate very high incomes.

Unit of analysis: taxpayer, in aggregated tabulations by income bracket, sex, age, federative unit, and other available classifications  
Periodicity: annual  
Access: public, in aggregated data and open statistics
</small><br><br/></div>

**PNS, Pesquisa Nacional de Saúde, IBGE e Ministério da Saúde** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('PNS')">More</a> | [Site][PNSsite] ] <br/>

<div id="PNS" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
National household survey with information on health conditions, chronic diseases, use of health services, lifestyles, morbidity, access to care, and work related health characteristics. It is widely used in health economics, economic demography, regional inequalities, and public policy evaluation.

Unit of analysis: individual, household, and selected resident, depending on the module  
Periodicity: specific rounds  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**CadÚnico** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('CADUNICO')">More</a> | [Site][CADUNICOsite] ] <br/>

<div id="CADUNICO" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Administrative registry of low income families, widely used in studies of targeting, poverty, social protection, and program evaluation.

Unit of analysis: family and individual  
Periodicity: continuous administrative updating  
Access: restricted for identifiable microdata, with some public tabulations and derived public databases
</small><br><br/></div>

</small><br><br/></div>

Administrative database of formal hirings and dismissals. It is widely used in short run labor market analysis and in studies of employment dynamics.

Unit of analysis: formal employment tie  
Periodicity: monthly  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**DATASUS, TabNet, Ministério da Saúde** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('DATASUS')">More</a> | [Site][DATASUSsite] ] <br/>

<div id="DATASUS" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Health information system with data on mortality, births, hospitalizations, outpatient production, and other topics. It is widely used in health economics, public policy evaluation, and regional studies.

Unit of analysis: aggregated administrative records, depending on the system consulted  
Periodicity: varies according to the database  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**SIM, Sistema de Informações sobre Mortalidade, Ministério da Saúde** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SIM')">More</a> | [Site][SIMsite] ] <br/>

<div id="SIM" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
National system of death records, with information on overall mortality, underlying causes of death, and demographic characteristics of individuals. It is widely used in economic demography, health economics, lethal violence, avoidable mortality, demographic transition, and territorial inequalities.

Unit of analysis: death record  
Periodicity: continuous updating, with periodic consolidation  
Access: public, with institutional system access and official data and tabulations made available in public portals
</small><br><br/></div>

**SINASC, Sistema de Informações sobre Nascidos Vivos, Ministério da Saúde** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SINASC')">More</a> | [Site][SINASCsite] ] <br/>

<div id="SINASC" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
National system of live birth records, with information on the newborn, the mother, pregnancy, and childbirth. It is widely used in economic demography, fertility, maternal and child health, regional inequalities, and public policy evaluation focused on early childhood.

Unit of analysis: live birth  
Periodicity: continuous updating, with periodic consolidation  
Access: public, with institutional system access and official data and tabulations made available in public portals
</small><br><br/></div>

</small><br><br/></div>

<div id="CENSOESC" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Annual census of Brazilian basic education, with information on schools, managers, classes, enrollments, students, and school staff. It is widely used in economics of education, regional economics, territorial inequalities, educational supply, and public policy evaluation.

Unit of analysis: school, class, enrollment, student, and school staff, depending on the database  
Periodicity: annual  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**SAEB, Sistema de Avaliação da Educação Básica, Inep** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SAEB')">More</a> | [Site][SAEBsite] ] <br/>

<div id="SAEB" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Large scale external assessment system aimed at measuring student performance and factors associated with learning. It is widely used in economics of education, educational policy evaluation, human capital, and the analysis of inequalities in school performance.

Unit of analysis: student, school, class, and school network, depending on the release  
Periodicity: periodic, according to the assessment cycle  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**IDEB, Índice de Desenvolvimento da Educação Básica, Inep** <br/>
[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('IDEB')">More</a> | [Site][IDEBsite] ] <br/>

<div id="IDEB" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
Composite indicator that combines school progression, obtained from Censo Escolar, and performance in SAEB assessments. It is widely used in economics of education, monitoring educational quality, regional comparison, and analysis of results by school network, educational stage, and school unit.

Unit of analysis: school, municipality, state, school network, and Brazil  
Periodicity: periodic, according to the release of results  
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

# <center> Main Economics Meetings in Brazil </center>
- - -


**(ANPEC) Encontro Nacional de Economia**, *da Associação Nacional dos Centros de Pósgraduação em Economia* <br/> 
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('ANPEC')">More</a> | [Site][ANPEC] ] </small>

<div id="ANPEC" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
The Encontro Nacional de Economia, organized by the Associação Nacional dos Centros de Pósgraduação em Economia, is one of the main academic meetings in Economics in Brazil. It typically gathers research in microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, economic development, public economics, labor economics, international economics, and applied fields more broadly. It is a key venue for faculty members, researchers, and graduate students.
 
</small><br><br/></div>

[ANPEC]: https://en.anpec.org.br/


**(ANPEC-Sul) Encontro de Economia da Região Sul**, *da Associação Nacional dos Centros de Pósgraduação em Economia* <br/> 
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('ANPECS')">More</a> | [Site][ANPECS] ] </small>

<div id="ANPECS" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
The Encontro de Economia da Região Sul is an important regional academic meeting. It usually includes research on regional development, labor markets, agricultural economics, public policy, economic history, and applied quantitative methods, with strong participation from researchers based in Southern Brazil.
 
</small><br><br/></div>

[ANPECS]: https://www.anpec.org.br/novosite/br/xxix-encontro-de-economia-da-regiao-sul 


**(ANPEC-NE) Encontro Regional de Economia do Nordeste**, *da Associação Nacional dos Centros de Pósgraduação em Economia* <br/> 
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('ANPECNE')">More</a> | [Site][ANPECNE] ] </small>

<div id="ANPECNE" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
The Encontro Regional de Economia do Nordeste is a major forum focused on economic issues related to the Northeast of Brazil. Common topics include regional inequality, economic development, poverty, labor markets, public policy, rural dynamics, productive structure, and social indicators.
 
</small><br><br/></div>

[ANPECNE]:  https://www.anpec.org.br/novosite/br/xxxi-encontro-regional-de-economia 


**(ABER) Encontro Nacional da Associação Brasileira de Estudos Regionais e Urbanos** <br/> 
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('ABER')">More</a> | [Site][ABER] ] </small>

<div id="ABER" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
The Encontro Nacional da Associação Brasileira de Estudos Regionais e Urbanos is a leading meeting for regional and urban economics. It commonly addresses regional development, urbanization, spatial inequality, housing, transport, urban and regional planning, territorial policies, and spatial econometrics.
 
</small><br><br/></div>

[ABER]:  https://brsa.org.br/encontros/ 


**(ABEP) Encontro Nacional de Estudos Populacionais**, *da Associação Brasileira de Estudos Populacionais* <br/> 
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('ABEP')">More</a> | [Site][ABEP] ] </small>

<div id="ABEP" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2" ><small>
The Encontro Nacional de Estudos Populacionais is one of the main Brazilian meetings in demography, with strong connections to economics and the social sciences. It covers topics such as fertility, mortality, migration, aging, family dynamics, labor markets, demographic transition, survey methods, and the relationship between population dynamics and public policy.
 
</small><br><br/></div>

[ABEP]: https://www.abep.org.br/abep/page/258-home

The Encontro Nacional de Economia Política is an important forum for political economy and heterodox approaches. It includes research on capitalism, economic crises, development, the State, public policy, history of economic thought, inequality, labor, and contemporary transformations in the global economy.
 
</small><br><br/></div>

The Encontro Nacional da Associação Brasileira de Estudos do Trabalho is a major academic meeting dedicated to labor studies. It typically includes research on labor markets, employment, informality, unions, labor law, social protection, platform work, gender and race inequalities, and structural changes in labor relations.
 
</small><br><br/></div>
