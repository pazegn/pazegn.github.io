# <center> Data Guide </center>
- - -


<p style="text-align: justify;">
This section brings together selected databases for research in Economics, organized into thematic blocks. The guide prioritizes broad official systems and sources that are especially useful for studies in Regional and Urban Economics, Economic Demography, Labor Economics and Migration, and Environmental and Ecological Economics.
</p>

<br/>

<h3><a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('block1')" style="text-decoration: none;">▸ 1. General, demographic, and socioeconomic databases</a></h3>

<div id="block1" style="display: none;">

**PNAD Contínua, IBGE** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('PNADC')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.ibge.gov.br/estatisticas/sociais/trabalho/2511-np-pnad-continua.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="PNADC" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Household sample survey with information on labor, income, education, demographic characteristics, and housing conditions. It is widely used in labor economics, inequality, poverty, and public policy evaluation.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: individual and household<br/>
Periodicity: continuous, with monthly, quarterly, and annual releases, depending on the indicator<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**Censo Demográfico, IBGE** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('CENSO')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.ibge.gov.br/estatisticas/sociais/populacao/2098-np-censo-demografico.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="CENSO" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Population and housing census of Brazil. It is a classic database for studies in economic demography, regional economics, urbanization, labor markets, and spatial inequality.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: individual, household, and territorial units<br/>
Periodicity: decennial<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**POF, Pesquisa de Orçamentos Familiares, IBGE** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('POF')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.ibge.gov.br/pof2024/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="POF" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Household survey focused on expenditure structure, income, consumption, nutrition, and living conditions. It is widely used in studies on consumption, welfare, poverty, demand elasticities, and food economics.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: household, individual, and consumption unit<br/>
Periodicity: non continuous, carried out in specific rounds<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**SIDRA, IBGE** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SIDRA')">More</a> | <a href="https://sidra.ibge.gov.br/acervo" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="SIDRA" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
IBGE system for retrieving statistical tables. It gathers aggregated results from economic, social, and demographic surveys and is especially useful for historical series, territorial comparisons, and the construction of municipal, state, and national panels.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: statistical aggregates, depending on the survey consulted<br/>
Periodicity: varies according to the statistical operation<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**IPEAData, Ipea** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('IPEADATA')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.ipeadata.gov.br/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="IPEADATA" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Broad database of economic, social, regional, and financial time series, widely used in applied research in Brazil. It is frequently used for inflation, economic activity, labor market, public accounts, and social indicators.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: aggregated series, with national, regional, state, and municipal breakdowns, depending on the indicator<br/>
Periodicity: varies according to the series<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**SGS, Sistema Gerenciador de Séries Temporais, Banco Central do Brasil** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('SGS')">More</a> | <a href="https://www4.bcb.gov.br/pec/series/port/aviso.asp?frame=1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="SGS" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Economic and financial time series database maintained by the Central Bank of Brazil. It is widely used in macroeconomics, monetary economics, external sector analysis, credit, interest rates, inflation, and public finance.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: aggregated series<br/>
Periodicity: daily, monthly, quarterly, or annual, depending on the series<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**IRPF, Imposto de Renda da Pessoa Física, Receita Federal do Brasil** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('IRPF')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.gov.br/receitafederal/pt-br/acesso-a-informacao/dados-abertos/grandes-numeros-do-irpf" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="IRPF" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Tax database with aggregated information from personal income tax returns. It is widely used in studies of inequality, income concentration, top incomes, and tax analysis, especially as a complement to household surveys, which tend to understate very high incomes.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: taxpayer, in aggregated tabulations by income bracket, sex, age, federative unit, and other available classifications<br/>
Periodicity: annual<br/>
Access: public, in aggregated data and open statistics
</small><br><br/></div>

**PNS, Pesquisa Nacional de Saúde, IBGE e Ministério da Saúde** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('PNS')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.ibge.gov.br/estatisticas/sociais/saude/9160-pesquisa-nacional-de-saude.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="PNS" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
National household survey with information on health conditions, chronic diseases, use of health services, lifestyles, morbidity, access to care, and work related health characteristics. It is widely used in health economics, economic demography, regional inequalities, and public policy evaluation.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: individual, household, and selected resident, depending on the module<br/>
Periodicity: specific rounds<br/>
Access: public
</small><br><br/></div>

**CadÚnico** <br/>
<small>[<a href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="visib('CADUNICO')">More</a> | <a href="https://www.gov.br/mds/pt-br/acoes-e-programas/cadastro-unico" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Site</a> ]</small>

<div id="CADUNICO" style="display: none; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.2"><small>
Administrative registry of low income families, widely used in studies of targeting, poverty, social protection, and program evaluation.<br/><br/>
Unit of analysis: family and individual<br/>
Periodicity: continuous administrative updating<br/>
Access: restricted for identifiable microdata, with some public tabulations and derived public databases
</small><br><br/></div>
